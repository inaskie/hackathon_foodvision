Concept

Step 1: Pre-Selection

Each participating country holds national pre-selections where aspiring chefs and home cooks showcase their culinary talents. Create your signature dish, film your story, and submit it to your national selection.

Step 2: National Selection

From the pre-selection pool, each country selects ONE representative to compete at the FoodVision Grand Finale. These finalists represent the best of their nation's culinary spirit.

Step 3: The Grand Finale

Nine finalists. One stage. One unforgettable night. Each chef creates a fusion dish combining local Fulda/Rhön ingredients with European elements from their home country.

Voting System

FoodVision uses a dual voting system to crown the winner, ensuring both culinary excellence and public engagement shape the outcome.

60% Professional Jury

Expert chefs, food historians, and culinary professionals evaluate dishes on taste, creativity, sustainability, and storytelling.

40% Public Vote

Viewers across Europe vote via SMS to support their favorite dish, giving everyone a voice in the competition.
